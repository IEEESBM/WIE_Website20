HOW TO TAKE MEMBERSHIP



If you wish to join a global community of IEEE members who are interested in promoting women in all STEM fields you are welcome to become a part of WIE to inspire young girls to pave their own way in the fields of science and engineering. IEEE WIE membership is offered to all IEEE members. WIE is free for Students, Graduate Student Members, and Life Members.





WIE MEMBER

A member forms the heart of Women in Engineering. Becoming a member can give you a head-start to change and development of women in STEM fields. There are several perks in being a WIE member in addition to the original IEEE membership benefits. Get a chance to work with other professionals in your specific work of interest, to receive resources on latest developments in technology and much more!



Volunteers are an essential part of the IEEE organization, and IEEE WIE offers many ways to get involved. If you are interested in volunteering with IEEE WIE, the best place to get started is volunteering at your nearest the affinity groups. Volunteer opportunities include coordinating events, mentoring, or serving as an officer.
