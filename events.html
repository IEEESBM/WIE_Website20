WIE INAUGURATION

The WIE Affinity Group Inaugural session was conducted on 6th September,2020 at 5pm through the online mode using Cisco Webex meeting. The Chief Guests invited for this ceremony were Mrs Sadhana Attavar, Chair of WIE Bangalore section and Mr Puneet Kumar Mishra , Chairperson of IEEE Bangalore Section.The guest of honour was Mrs Jayanthi Prabhu and we had Dr Vinod V Thomas, Registrar Evaluation MAHE preside over the session .
The chief guest Mrs Sadhana Attavar inaugurated the affinity group and congratulated us. She then addressed everyone and gave a brief talk about WIE, about its establishment and its vision and goals. She told us about the working, various competitions and tasks undertaken by WIE and motivated us all to work for this wonderful chapter.
