OUR PROJECTS

WIE WEBSITE

The team of the WIE Affinity Group of IEEE Student Branch Manipal undertook the project of designing a website for WIE IEEE- SBM in the month of September. The team consisted of a total of 10 members who worked under the guidance of 4 Board members from IEEE SBM. The website was designed using CSS, HTML and JavaScript.

The WebDev team had 8 members. 7 from IEEE SBM ManComm guided by the WebMaster of IEEE SBM Board 20-21. They worked and learned together the different aspects of Graphics and Web Development as they ultimately delivered great results. The UI/UX designing for the website was done by Anushree Bhat of the WebDev team.

There was also a dedicated team for content who provided the articles for the WIE blog and other content material for the different pages of the website. It was a great learning opportunity for all the members as they got to learn and build on the skills of website development and team work.

We would like to thank the WebMaster, Abhishek Srivastav and Graphics Head, Ashish Sharma, IEEE SBM Board 2020-21 for their guidance and support in this project and the Chairperson and Vice Chairperson of WIE Affinty Group Navya Ambardar and Prita Saha for taking the initiative.
