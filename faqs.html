Q1. How do I join IEEE WIE

If you are already an IEEE Member: Log in with your IEEE Account on the official website and add IEEE Women in Engineering Membership If you are a non member then you need to first take IEEE membership and then follow the above guideline. You must be an IEEE member to become a WIE Member

Q2. Is WIE membership only entitled to women

One of the common misconceptions people have about WIE is if it is restricted to only women. No, WIE membership can be taken by any IEEE member ,be it a male or female.

Q3. How to volunteer for IEEE WIE

One can volunteer by coordinating events, mentoring, or serving as an officer in the various WIE Events which take place in your local area. Contact your local WIE Affinity Group to know more about the volunteer opportunities.

Q4. What type of funding is available for IEEE WIE Student Affinity Groups

A limited amount of annual funding is available to support local WIE activities that are of educational and professional value to IEEE members. Newly established affinity groups get a funding of USD $200 and USD $400 for special projects for established affinity groups.

Q5. How to receive the IEEE WIE Newsletter

The IEEE WIE Newsletter, known as IEEE WIE Exchange is issued on a bi-monthly basis(February, April, June, August, October, December) to IEEE WIE Members. It is distributed to the email address linked with IEEE while taking the membership. The past Newsletters can be issued by sending a request to woman@ieee.org by WIE Members only.

Q6. How can I obtain promotional materials for a WIE event

WIE offers promotional items for WIE Affinity Group events and activities to bring visibility to IEEE Women in Engineering. The quantities are limited and items should be supplemental to your primary source of promotional/items for your event.

Q7. How can I purchase IEEE WIE Volunteer recognition products

IEEE Volunteer Recognition products such as banners, certificates, pins and plaques are available through IEEE. This will be done by the respective IEEE Student Branch.

Q8. How do I join the women in Leadership Community

IEEE Collabratec is an online platform that allows women engineers, scientists, and educators from around the world to network, connect and collaborate with individuals from various technical backgrounds.
